About Rebecca

Rebecca Skier is a currently a sophomore at Syracuse University. She is an aspiring UX/UI designer, with a dual major in Graphic Design and Information Management Technology. Rebecca is heavily experienced with working in Adobe Illustrator, and also has a experience with Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Xd, Adobe InDesign, HTML and CSS. Rebecca specializes in typeography, as well as layout and poster design. She is always looking for new challenges and oppurtunities to grow her skills. If you would like to get in touch, please do not hesitate to visit Rebecca's contact page.
